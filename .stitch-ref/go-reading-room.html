Government of Andhra Pradesh

Abstract

FINANCE (HR.I-Plg. & Policy) DEPARTMENT - Public Services - Implementation of 11th Pay Revision Commission - Master Scale - Revised Scales of Pay 2022 - Orders - Issued.

G.O.Ms.No.1 Dated: 17-01-2022

Read the following:

  1. G.O.Ms.No.75, General Administration (SC.A) Department, dated 28.05.2018.
  2. G.O.Rt.No.75, General Administration (SC.A) Department, dated 02.06.2018.
  3. Report of the 11th Pay Revision Commission submitted on 05.10.2020.
***

Order:

The Government of Andhra Pradesh constituted the 11th Pay Revision Commission vide the orders first read above. The Commission submitted its report to the Government on 05.10.2020. Government have constituted a Committee of Secretaries vide G.O.Rt.No.1420, General Administration (SC.A) Department, dated 25.06.2021 to examine the recommendations of the 11th Pay Revision Commission.

2. Government, after careful consideration of the recommendations of the 11th Pay Revision Commission and the report of the Committee of Secretaries, hereby order the implementation of the Revised Master Scale to the State Government Employees as detailed below.
